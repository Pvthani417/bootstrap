Semester system of instruction and evaluation was introduced in the degree Programmes in 1976 - 77. B.A. (Eng.) was revived in 1979-80. The College also enjoyed the fruits of College Sciences Improvement Programmes of UGC. M.Com. Programme was introduced in the academic year 1985-86 and B.Sc.
